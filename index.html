IEEE BIBM 2026 Workshop On

Explainable and Trustworthy AI for Clinical Decision Support Systems

This workshop brings together researchers and practitioners working at the intersection of explainable artificial intelligence, trustworthy machine learning, and clinical decision support. The approved program will focus on interpretable methods, real-world deployment, and the human and regulatory dimensions of AI in healthcare.

About

Clinical decision support systems are increasingly powered by machine learning and artificial intelligence, but predictive accuracy alone is not enough for real-world clinical adoption. This workshop addresses the need for transparency, trust, accountability, robustness, fairness, and clinically meaningful explanations.

Scope

  • Core themeExplainable AI for healthcare
  • EmphasisTrustworthy clinical deployment
  • AudienceAI, biomedical, and clinical communities
  • SettingBiomedical informatics and AI research

Workshop topics

Explainable AI methods

Approaches for clinical decision support systems, including model-agnostic and model-specific techniques.

Interpretable healthcare models

Machine learning methods for healthcare applications, including deep learning and transparent model design.

Medical imaging and EHRs

Explainability for imaging, electronic health records, and multimodal medical data.

Ethics and regulation

Bias, fairness, regulatory considerations, and responsible deployment in clinical environments.
